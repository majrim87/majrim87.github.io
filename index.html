What is it?

Feeder will help you decide where and what you want to eat based on your location, your price range and what you are in the mood for (like witch type of food or a specific dish).

For who?

The app can be used by anyone who wants to eat out but doesn’t know what or where to eat or what to order in a specific restorant, mostly teens and adults.
